COVID relief work dashboard

Project for Tata Institute of Social Science's Community Led Action and Participation Project. The dashboard tracks covid relief effors undertaken during the second wave of COVID in Mumbai in Mar-Jun 2021.

Vulnerability Index mapping

Project for Tata Institute of Social Science's Community Led Action and Participation Project. The project conducted a survey of 25,000 households in Mumbai's M-East Ward. The dashboard summarises results of the vulnerability index created to from the survey data.

Repository of Mumbai's spatial datasets

Repository containing Mumbai's spatial boundary data on electoral wards (parliamentary, assembly and local body), administrative boundaries, census and basti (informal settlement) boundaries.
